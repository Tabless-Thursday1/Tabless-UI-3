What We Do

Save Tabs

With Tabless Thursday, you are now able to save all of your tabs on a single page where they are ready to fire! You no longer have to look through all of your tabs (and in many cases, windows!) to pick up where you left off.

Categorize Tabs

Organize your tabs by putting them into categories. A powerful tool for people that need lots of resources to get a lot done. You can always delete what you don't need. Find what you need to use quicker!

Save Memory

Tabless Thursday will reduce your computer's memory usage which will improve your computers speed. This is especially useful for tabs that are always open but are never used!
